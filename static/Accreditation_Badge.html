A Mark of Excellence in Kashmiri Handicrafts

What is the HCRF Badge?

The HCRF Badge is a prestigious certification awarded by the Hamdan Craft Revival Foundation (HCRF) to artisans and businesses in the Kashmiri handicraft industry. This badge serves as a mark of authenticity, quality, and ethical craftsmanship. It signals to consumers and the global market that the products are genuine, traditionally made, and adhere to the highest standards of sustainability and fair trade.

Earning the HCRF Badge allows artisans and businesses to gain recognition for their commitment to preserving Kashmiri cultural heritage and maintaining ethical production practices.

Showcasing Excellence in Craftsmanship, Sustainability, & Ethical Practices

Types of HCRF Accreditation Badges

HCRF Accreditation Badges recognize businesses and artisans committed to preserving the heritage of Kashmiri craftsmanship while promoting sustainable and ethical practices. Each badge highlights a different area of excellence, helping businesses stand out in the global market and build consumer trust.

Authentic Craftsmanship Badge

Recognizes artisans who maintain the integrity of traditional Kashmiri craftsmanship, focusing on quality, authenticity, and heritage preservation.

Ethical Trade and Fair Wages Badge

This badge honors artisans and businesses committed to promoting ethical trade practices, ensuring artisans receive fair wages and respect.

Sustainable Practices Badge

Awarded to those practicing environmentally conscious craftsmanship, using sustainable materials and methods to reduce their environmental footprint.

Cultural Preservation Badge

This badge celebrates efforts that protect and preserve Kashmir’s cultural heritage, ensuring traditional skills pass to future generations.

Innovative Craft Design Badge

Recognizes artisans blending creativity with tradition, introducing fresh, modern designs while keeping the essence of Kashmiri craftsmanship alive.

Women Empowerment Badge

Acknowledges initiatives supporting women artisans, providing them with opportunities for skill development, leadership, and financial independence.

Innovation in Craft Technology Badge

Highlights artisans or businesses using modern technology to enhance production, streamline processes, or introduce technological advancements in crafting.

Community Development Badge

Honors contributions toward the social and economic upliftment of artisan communities, promoting growth, self-reliance, and empowerment through crafts.

Craft Research Badge

Recognizes significant research contributions in the craft sector, fostering knowledge, innovation, and development within the Kashmiri craft industry.
