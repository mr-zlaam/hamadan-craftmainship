Cancellation and Refund Policy

General Terms:

HCRF charges fees for membership, donations, and e-publications. These services are non-refundable once processed.

Cancellation and refund requests apply only to applicable services as outlined below.

Cancellations:

Requests for cancellations must be made within 7 working days of purchase.

Cancellations are not possible once the service has been initiated or fulfilled.

Refund Process:

Refunds, where applicable, will be processed within 7 working days of approval to the original payment method.

Non-Refundable Services:

Membership, donations, and e-publications are non-refundable once processed.

Warranty and Product Issues:

HCRF does not deal with physical products, so warranty-related issues do not apply.
